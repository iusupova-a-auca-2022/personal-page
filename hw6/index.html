Who We Are

More Than a Club — A Movement

AUCA Women is the official community platform for students, alumni, faculty, and staff who identify as women at the American University of Central Asia in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.

We create safe spaces for dialogue, celebrate achievements, facilitate mentorships, and connect generations of AUCA women who are shaping Central Asia and beyond.
